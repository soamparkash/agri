What are Fruit Crops?

Fruit crops are perennial or seasonal horticultural crops grown primarily for human consumption. They are rich sources of vitamins, minerals, fiber, and antioxidants and play a key role in nutrition security and farmer income.

Climatic Requirements

  • Varied climate depending on fruit type
  • Sub-tropical and tropical regions dominate fruit production
  • Well-drained fertile soils required
  • Regular irrigation and pruning improve yield

Importance of Fruit Crops

Fruit cultivation supports agro-based industries, exports, and value-added processing. India is one of the largest producers of fruits like mango, banana, and citrus in the world.

Major Fruit Crops in India

Mango Banana Apple Orange Grapes Papaya Pineapple Pomegranate
