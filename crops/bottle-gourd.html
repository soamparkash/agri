General Information

Bottle gourd, popularly known as Lauki, is a fast-growing Zaid vegetable crop widely cultivated in India. It is valued for its nutritional benefits, easy digestibility, and medicinal properties. Bottle gourd performs well in warm climates and is suitable for trellis cultivation.

  • Scientific Name: Lagenaria siceraria
  • Season: Zaid
  • Climate: Warm and humid

Soil Requirement

Bottle gourd grows best in fertile, well-drained sandy loam soils rich in organic matter. Water stagnation can cause root rot.
