General Information

Cannabis is a plant species known for its psychoactive and industrial uses. While the recreational use of cannabis is illegal in India, certain parts of the plant such as hemp (low THC varieties) may be cultivated under government license for industrial and research purposes in specific states.

  • Scientific Name: Cannabis sativa
  • Plant Type: Annual herb
  • General Climate: Warm temperate
  • Natural Growth Season: Kharif & Rabi (varies)

Traditional & Industrial Uses

  • Fiber production (hemp) for ropes, textiles, paper
  • Seeds used for oil extraction (industrial use)
  • Medicinal research (under government approval)
  • Traditional uses mentioned in ancient Indian texts

Legal Status in India

Under the Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances (NDPS) Act, 1985:

  • Recreational cultivation is illegal
  • Licensed cultivation of industrial hemp is allowed in some states
  • Medical research is permitted with government approval

Important Note

Farmers and individuals should not cultivate cannabis without proper legal authorization. Unauthorized cultivation can result in severe legal penalties including fines and imprisonment.
