General Information

Barley is an important Rabi cereal crop grown mainly in dry and marginal areas of India. It is used for food, animal feed, malting, and brewing. Barley is more tolerant to drought and saline soils compared to wheat.

  • Scientific Name: Hordeum vulgare
  • Season: Rabi
  • Climate: Cool and dry

Soil Requirement

Barley can be grown on a wide range of soils, including saline and alkaline soils. However, well-drained loamy soils give the best yields.

  • Method: Line sowing with seed drill
