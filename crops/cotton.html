General Information

Cotton is one of the most important commercial crops of India and is grown mainly for its fiber. India is among the leading producers of cotton in the world. Cotton requires a warm climate and a long frost-free period.

  • Scientific Name: Gossypium spp.
  • Season: Kharif
  • Climate: Warm and dry

Soil Requirement

Cotton grows best in deep, well-drained soils with good moisture retention. Black cotton soil is considered ideal.

  • Best soils: Black cotton soil, loam
  • Ideal pH range: 6.0 ‚Äì 8.0
