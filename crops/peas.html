General Information

Peas, commonly known as Matar, are an important Rabi pulse and vegetable crop in India. They are rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals and are grown both for fresh green pods and dry seeds. Peas also improve soil fertility by fixing atmospheric nitrogen.

  • Scientific Name: Pisum sativum
  • Season: Rabi
  • Climate: Cool and moist
  • Temperature: 10‚Äì20¬∞C
  • Rainfall: 40‚Äì60 cm

Soil Requirement

Peas grow best in fertile, well-drained loamy soils. Heavy clay soils and waterlogged conditions should be avoided as they promote root diseases.

  • Best soils: Loam, sandy loam
  • Ideal pH range: 6.0 ‚Äì 7.5
