Role of Fertilizers in Agriculture

Fertilizers and agricultural inputs play a vital role in enhancing soil fertility, improving crop productivity, and ensuring sustainable agricultural practices. They provide essential macro and micro nutrients required for healthy plant growth.

Proper selection and balanced use of fertilizers helps increase yield, maintain soil health, and reduce environmental impact while supporting food security.
