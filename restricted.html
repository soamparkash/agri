About Restricted & Illegal Crops

Certain crops in India are classified as restricted or illegal due to their potential misuse, narcotic properties, or impact on public health and safety. Cultivation of these crops is governed by strict laws and regulations. This section is provided strictly for educational and awareness purposes.

Legal Disclaimer

Cultivation, possession, or trade of restricted crops without proper authorization is a punishable offense under Indian law. Always consult government guidelines and obtain necessary licenses before engaging in any activity related to such crops.
